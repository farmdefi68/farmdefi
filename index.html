FEATURES

FARM Me is a fantasy metaverse exploring all aspects of "Play, Connect, Experience, Earn" for multiplayer to experience wholeset of the first immersive farm game with a battle-to-survive element.

Connect with Your Friends

Free to play

Opening job opportunities by smart contract agreement between investors & players:

An investor may hire another to work.

A player can rent other NFT items for playing.

Play to save

In games, buy a mystery gift for the designated relatives/friends.

Together, contribute to community funds (FMCF - Farm Me Community Fund).

Farm Me cares about user experience:

PVP player: combat, death match or capture the flag,...PVE player: solo and co-op mode.

Event: daily, weekly, monthly and seasonal events...

Minigame: fruit cutting, racing puzzles...

In Farm Me, you are not alone:

Town Island is where gamers meet to chat, trade and gift to friends.

Players can organize weddings and create the next generation of Heroes.

Players can discuss game activities, find new friends in the decentralized forum.

Show off personal color palette:

The Fashion feature makes your Hero not only unique, but also stronger in base perfomance.

Architecture: constantly innovating to create your own home.

Crafting: unleash your imagination with hundreds of recipes to design useful and beautiful items.

An adventure without end...

Meet & connecting with new friends.

Own mythical pets by playing the role of a hunter at Hunting Island.

Kill bosses for rewards and hunt for rare items with 'Friend Power".

A true metaverse citizen

Choose 1 of 6 character classes, such as Farmer, Agent, Police, Soldier, Special Force, Super Hero.

Farm and breed on the land that you already own.

Fish & mine to increase income.

Cook from your farm produce to create dishes that increase Hero's stats.

Complete daily tasks to receive rewards.

Virtual Island Chains

Farm Me universe contains a group of the main islands and floating ones with specific roles. Each one provides a magical feature and plays an important role in the whole of the metaverse.

Game Activities

- Sow seeds, water, fertilize, harvest vegetables and fruits to sell to developers to increase income.

- A Rare Item may be dropped throughout the harvesting process.

- There are dozens of different crops to explore throughout the game.

- There are six different types of livestock and poultry (Swine, Cow, Chicken, Goat, Goose, Sheep) to diversify agricultural products.

- Farm products can be sold directly or crafted into higher-value products to sell for $ME.

- Cattle and poultry of the same breed can be cross-bred to produce gene mutations with higher economic value.

There are hundreds of delicious recipes hidden in the game.

-The house simply needs an upgrade to include a Kitchen and Kitchen Tools so that you may prepare delicious meals.

- The best part is that the food can raise Hero's stat.

Do you want to supplement your income? Come over to the job platform.

- Those with extra cash but not enough time can publish a job advertisement for support staff. People with low money can go to work to get richer.

- There are various kinds of pets and eggs that players may obtain to help them in the process of setting up their farm.

- Make your hero standout out by dressing him in a variety of outfits.

- Rare costumes can boost Hero's base stats (HP, SP, ATK, etc.)

- A place for gamers to exchange and trade items with each other.

- This Fame Me universe offers the opportunity to become wealthy if you perform well.

- There are hundreds of recipes for making helpful and beautiful items.

- Players explore the hunting ground to battle legendary creatures from all over the world.

- Wild creatures can be tamed and turned into pets for players who are lucky enough.

- Because the map is so huge, there will be a transit system ranging from bicycles to automobiles to assist Heroes in getting around this new planet more quickly.

- There are many types of ores to mine.

- These ores can be used to upgrade Farm Tools or as materials to craft unique items.

Players compete against one another to win $FAME tokens and other unique items.
